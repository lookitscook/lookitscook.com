For 2008 and 2009 I implemented a complete redesign of the site using XHTML/CSS. In addition to this I created a live webcast player to broadcast a feed during the event. Onsite support was provided during the conference to ensure smooth operation. For the international registration system I also authored an i18n translation system in ColdFusion 8 and T-SQL to enable the page content to be displayed in multiple languages.
